description=[h1]Safehouse Inventory[/h1]
Turn your base into one tidy, searchable stash — and never haul home a fourth hammer you didn't need again.

[i]Like this? Also grab [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3740165370]Proximity Inventory[/url] — and [url=https://github.com/DevL0rd/Zomboid-BaseInventory]contribute / report bugs on GitHub[/url].[/i]

[b]Safehouse Inventory[/b] adds a single tab to the loot window that gathers everything stored across your whole safehouse into one list. Crates, shelves, fridges, wardrobes, every floor from the basement to the attic — all in one place. Browse it, take from it, and even cook and craft straight out of your entire base while you're home.

[h2]What it does[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]One tab, your whole base.[/b] Every container in your safehouse, merged into a single inventory.
[*][b]All floors included.[/b] Basement, ground floor, that hoarder attic — all counted (toggleable per zone).
[*][b]Stays put.[/b] The tab keeps itself selected as you wander and rummage, instead of snapping away the moment you glance at another container.
[*][b]Cook & craft from the whole base.[/b] Recipes pull from every container in the safehouse, not just the one you're standing at.
[/list]

[h2]The "do I already have this?" badge[/h2]
Out scavenging? Any item you already keep at your safehouse gets a little house badge — anywhere in the world, in any container, on the floor, even in your own bags. Hover it and a tooltip tells you which safehouse has it, how many, and the total across all of them. No more mystery hoarding.

[h2]Claim your home — even solo[/h2]
Safehouses normally only work in multiplayer. This mod quietly patches them to work in [b]single-player[/b] too: walk into a building, right-click, [b]Claim Safehouse[/b], done. (Bonus: other mods that lean on safehouse mechanics get to work in solo play as well.)

[h2]Share with your crew[/h2]
Playing together? Your safehouse zones are shared with the people you add as safehouse members — your stash, your rules, your roommates.

[h2]Plays nice[/h2]
Works with vanilla and is friendly with CleanUI. Single-player and multiplayer both supported.
